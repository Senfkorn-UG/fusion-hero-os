=== TITLE ===
Autopoiesis and Autopolitics of Fusion Hero OS

=== AUTHORS ===
Stephan Hagen Urban

=== ABSTRACT (paste) ===
This dissertation (monograph v1.0) formulates Fusion Hero OS as both an autopoietic system—continuously producing the components that maintain it (MasterSeed, CEC, Sisyphos regeneration, fractal mesh, archives, tests)—and an autopolitical constitution that self-legislates placement (L0–L4), consent, connector membranes, and proof honesty. Cross-reference: bifocality of universe ↔ brain with the Standard Model of particle physics (SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1)) as cosmic-side frame for law-like closure under controlled symmetry breaking; the brain-side counterpart is self-producing cognitive–operational closure. No physical identity of brain and universe is claimed—only a heuristic isomorphism (OPEN in the proof registry), analogous to bifurcal mesh pull/push and MasterSeed fixed-point logic. Drawing on systems theory, existential philosophy, and live infrastructure empirics (mesh coordination, dashboard health, academia curriculum alignment), it argues that heroic eudaimonia is recursive stability under controlled rupture, not a happiness sum. Code-honesty constraints (PROVEN/OPEN claims) are epistemic organs of the system rather than external critique.
