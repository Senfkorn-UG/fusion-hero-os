=== TITLE ===
Autopoiesis and Autopolitics of Fusion Hero OS

=== AUTHORS ===
Stephan Hagen Urban

=== ABSTRACT (paste) ===
Ontological thesis: the entire Fusion Hero OS is the dissertation; the text (monograph, PDF, abstract) is only one form of its expression. The work runs as a system—code, mesh, dashboard, tests, archives, proof registry, and live operation are coequal organs, not illustrations of a prior essay. Autopoiesis is continuous self-production of system-essential components (MasterSeed, CEC, Sisyphos regeneration, fractal mesh, archives, tests). Autopolitics is the self-legislation of that closure: placement L0–L4, consent, connector membranes, and the separation of in-house truth from external SaaS memory. Bifocal cross-reference: universe ↔ brain with the Standard Model of particle physics (SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1)) as cosmic-side frame for law-like closure under controlled symmetry breaking; the brain/operative side is self-producing systemic closure (the OS itself). No physical identity of brain and universe is claimed—only a heuristic isomorphism (OPEN in the proof registry). Heroic eudaimonia is recursive stability under controlled rupture. Code-honesty constraints (PROVEN/OPEN/REFUTED) are epistemic organs of the work.
